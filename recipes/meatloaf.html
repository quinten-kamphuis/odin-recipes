Meatloaf

Description

Get back to basics with this easy meatloaf recipe. It's quick, traditional, delicious, and it's sure to please everyone at your table.

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ pounds ground beef
  • 1 egg
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 cup dried bread crumbs
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • ⅓ cup ketchup
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons prepared mustard

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven and prepare loaf pan.
  2. Combine ground beef, onion, milk, bread crumbs, and egg; season and transfer to pan.
  3. Mix ketchup, brown sugar, and mustard; pour over meatloaf.
  4. Bake until no longer pink in the center.
